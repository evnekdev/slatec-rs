# Purpose

This subprogram solves a linearly constrained least squares problem. Suppose there are given matrices E and A of respective dimensions ME by N and MA by N, and vectors F and B of respective lengths ME and MA. This subroutine solves the problem EX = F, (equations to be exactly satisfied) AX = B, (equations to be approximately satisfied, in the least squares sense) subject to components L+1,...,N nonnegative
