# Third-party notices for a future bundled provider

No historical SLATEC source or GNU runtime archive is included by this workspace. 1287 selected physical source units remain outside a distributable bundle pending source-specific provenance evidence.

When a source is cleared, its notice, licence statement, attribution, and hash-guarded evidence must be added to `crates/slatec-src/metadata/bundled-provenance-overrides.json` and regenerated before a carrier archive can be produced. GNU compiler-runtime notices are tracked separately from SLATEC source provenance.
